Perl 6 Noise Gang

Welcome to the Perl 6 Noise Gang

This is a group for the promotion and support of audio and music application development in Perl 6

What's this all about?

Perl 6 is an expressive and flexible programming language with a relatively simple mechanism to bind to native libraries, it seems a good choice for implementing certain types of audio and music applications, and it's fun too!

The aim of this group is to provide a focus for people writing sound oriented applications in Perl 6 and to help people find or use the tools and libraries that are already available. If there is stuff out there we want to bring it to people's attention.
